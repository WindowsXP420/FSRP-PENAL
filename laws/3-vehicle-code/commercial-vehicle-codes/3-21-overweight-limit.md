# \[3] 21 - Overweight Limit

When a Truck/Trailer exceeds a weight limit of **80,000 LBS** and is also **not** considered a legal oversized load.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Infraction**&#x20;

**Sentence:** None\
**Fine:** $200
{% endhint %}

**Note:**&#x20;

The Commercial Vehicle must be towed and impounded.&#x20;
